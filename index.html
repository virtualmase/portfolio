Resonance BFT · 3-Validator · Forward-Only Attestation

Resonance attests agent mandates rather than ordering transactions. Three validator nodes reach consensus on each action. The protocol adapts cycle frequency to network load, maintaining forward-only attestation with no re-orgs. All decisions are logged to the Truth Ledger with Merkle proofs.
